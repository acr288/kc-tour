Arroyo Burro Beach is named after the Arroyo Burro Creek that flows into the Pacific, but is more commonly known as Hendry's. Sometimes the names refer to two different sides of the beach. It is located below the Mesa and Douglas Family Preserve, which are ancient marine terraces. Hendry's is one of the most beautiful and beloved beaches in Santa Barbara.

Beaches extracted from the Domestic and Antarctic Names (State and Topical Gazetteer) published by the United States Board on Geographic Namkes. (geonames.usgs.gov) Data accessed June 3, 2021. Contours were extracted from a USGS NED 1/3 arc-second raster digital elevation model. This data can be accessed at the USGS 3D Elevation Program (usgs.gov)
